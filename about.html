A little About me

I’m majoring in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, and I’ve had an enriching experience learning about UI/UX and web development. Before college, I thought the design was simply an artistic skill. However, I’ve realized that design is much more than that - it’s about storytelling. As I craft meaningful and engaging user experiences, I often feel like a writer, drawing upon my ability to empathize with users and provide suitable solutions. I’m committed to creating designs that are accessible to all without any barriers.

I worked as a retail sales associate for five years, and received grateful feedback from customers about their experience with me. My strongest sales skill was empathizing with their needs and understanding their perspective, which I believe translates well into my work as a designer.

When I’m not designing, I love taking long walks without a destination. During these walks, I’ve had some of my most exceptional experiences, realizing that every path is connected, even when I get lost.
